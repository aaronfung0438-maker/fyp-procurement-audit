# Group-Specific Instructions — Group 1 (Statistics Only)

You have been assigned to **Group 1**.

## What You Will See

For every order, you will see:

- **Section A — Order Details**
- **Section B — Free-text Notes**
- **Section E — Deviation Sentences**

You will **not** see any AI conclusion or AI-generated commentary. Your
judgment must be based on the order details, the free-text notes, and
the comparison sentences in Section E.

## How to Approach the Task

Treat Section E as a starting point — it tells you which numerical
fields look unusual relative to the company's history. Combine that with
the qualitative information in Section B and your own common sense to
decide whether the order is **Normal** or **Suspicious**.

A *higher than usual* unit price or a *longer than usual* delivery lag
does not automatically mean the order is suspicious. You must judge
whether the deviation has a plausible business explanation, or whether
the combination of signals warrants concern.

## Practice Round

You will start with **2 practice questions** in this same format. After
each practice question you will see whether your decision was correct
(*Normal* / *Suspicious*) so you can calibrate your reading. The
practice answers are not used in the analysis.

## Reminder

- 32 official questions, no feedback during the official block.
- One submission per order. **No going back.**
- Aim for 45 to 75 seconds per question.

Press *Continue* to start the practice round.
